5-Day Superset Strength Workout Plan

A comprehensive strength-building program designed with diastasis recti safety in mind, featuring compound exercises and lower back focus without leg press or lying leg curl machines.

How to Use This Workout Plan

Navigate between workout days using the tabs below. Each day contains three supersets (A, B, C) with two exercises each. Click on exercises to view detailed instructions and safety notes. Use the weight input fields to track the weights you used for each exercise.

Workout Structure

  • • 3-4 sets per superset
  • • 8-12 reps per exercise
  • • 0-15 seconds rest between exercises
  • • 60-90 seconds rest between supersets

Safety First

  • • Monitor for abdominal doming
  • • Engage TVA before each exercise
  • • Focus on controlled breathing
  • • Progress gradually with proper form

Weight Tracking

Your weight entries are automatically saved in your browser and will persist between sessions.

Essential Safety Guidelines for Diastasis Recti

⚠️ Warning Signs to Watch For

  • • Abdominal doming or coning along the midline
  • • Pain or discomfort in the core area
  • • Inability to maintain proper form
  • • Excessive lower back arching

✅ Key Safety Practices

  • • Engage TVA (transverse abdominis) before each exercise
  • • Practice diaphragmatic breathing throughout
  • • Progress gradually with proper form over heavy weight
  • • Listen to your body and modify as needed

Important: This workout plan is for informational purposes only. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional, especially one specializing in postpartum recovery, before starting any new exercise program if you have diastasis recti.
